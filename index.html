Who We Are

RP BOOKS is a reputed book seller and supplier based in Kharagpur, Paschim Medinipur, West Bengal. We deal in a wide range of Indian and foreign books covering academic textbooks, competitive-exam guides, literature, fiction, non-fiction, children's books, and more.

Whether you are a student, teacher, institution, or an avid reader, we provide books at competitive prices with a commitment to quality and timely delivery. Our catalogue is regularly updated with new arrivals.
